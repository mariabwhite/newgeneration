04
Grammar Review

Your A1 grammar table

≈ 8 min
TopicFormulaExample
Present Simplesubject + verbI wake up at seven. She works at home.
QuestionsDo / Does + subject + base verb?Do you have a notebook? Does Alex wake up early?
Negativesdon't / doesn't + base verbI don't have a TV. Mia doesn't have a printer.
There is / There arethere is + one / there are + manyThere is a desk. There are two books.
Have / HasI/you/we/they have · he/she/it hasAlex has a bag. We have pencils.
Possessivesmy / your / his / her / our / their + nounIt is his bag. These are their keys.
Prepositionsin / on / under / next to / between / behind / in front ofThe bag is under the desk. The phone is next to the mug.
Instructionsput / move + object + placePut it on the desk. Move them next to the laptop.
Final rule: Keep A1 sentences simple. Use one clear idea in one sentence: person + action + object + place.

Final mistake check

Wrong: Does Alex has a bag? / This is I notebook. / Put them on desk.
Right: Does Alex have a bag? / This is my notebook. / Put them on the desk.
