What you will do in this lesson

в‰€ 3 min

In this lesson, you will learn to give simple instructions and polite requests. You will say where to put or move things. You will also use it for one thing and them for two or more things.

Start

You already know where things are: The bag is under the desk. Now you will give instructions: Put the bag under the desk.

Route

First look at objects, then learn action verbs, then practise commands, it / them, and polite requests.

Finish

At the end you will write or record 6вЂ“8 instructions for a room or workspace and ask 2 polite Can you...? questions.

Language Focus

Commands: put, move, take, find

в‰€ 8 min
ActionFormulaExample
PutPut + object + place.Put the notebook on the desk. Put the pens in the cup.
MoveMove + object + place.Move the chair next to the desk. Move the phone next to the coffee mug.
TakeTake + object + from + place.Take the keys from the bag. Take the notebook from the shelf.
FindFind + object + place.Find the charger under the notebook. Find the toy behind the box.
Polite requestCan you + action + object + place?Can you put the bag under the desk? Can you move the phone next to the mug?
Rule: Use the base verb in commands and after Can you. Do not use -s.
