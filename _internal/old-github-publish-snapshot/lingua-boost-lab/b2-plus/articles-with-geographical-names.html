🌍 ARTICLES WITH GEOGRAPHICAL NAMES

B2+ Masterclass | Ukraine · Crimea · Peninsulas · The "OF" Rule

🏆 THE GOLDEN RULE: “OF” CHANGES EVERYTHING
If a geographical name contains the preposition of, you almost always use the before the common noun.
📌 the Gulf of Mexico · the Strait of Gibraltar · the Republic of Korea · the Cape of Good Hope
⚠️ But: Lake Geneva (no 'of') → no article. The shores of Lake Baikal — still no 'the' before Lake Baikal.

📍 PART 2 — PARTS OF THE WORLD & PENINSULAS

No articleWith the
North America, Western Europe, Southeast Asiathe North, the South, the Middle East, the Arctic, the Balkans
Peninsulas: Kamchatka Peninsula, Iberian Peninsula, Korean Peninsula, Yucatán Peninsulathe Arabian Peninsula, the Indochinese Peninsula, the Peninsula of Kamchatka (rare)
🔹 Exception: the Crimean Peninsula (often with 'the' because of the adjective form). The Korean Peninsula can be used with or without 'the' in B2+ contexts.

🇺🇦 PART 3 — UKRAINE · CRIMEA · CITIES & REPUBLICS

No articleWith the
Ukraine (modern), France, Japan, Texas, London, Kyivthe USA, the Netherlands, the Philippines, the Hague, the Vatican
Crimea (as peninsula name)the Crimea (historical/dated), the Republic of Crimea, the Crimean Peninsula
—the Republic of Korea, the Kingdom of Denmark, the Russian Federation
📌 Ukraine: NO article (since 1991). Crimea: standard without 'the', but 'the Crimea' appears in historical texts. The Hague — always 'the'.

💧 PART 4 — RIVERS, LAKES, SEAS, STRAITS, CANALS

No articleWith the
Lake Baikal, Lake Geneva, Lake Michiganthe Great Lakes
—the Thames, the Dnieper, the Nile, the Amazon
San Francisco Bay (often without)the Mediterranean, the Black Sea, the Sea of Azov, the Kerch Strait, the Panama Canal
🌊 Complex B2+: The Dnieper flows through Kyiv into the Black Sea. The Kerch Strait connects the Black Sea to the Sea of Azov.

⛰️ PART 5 — MOUNTAINS, RANGES, DESERTS

No articleWith the
Mount Everest, Mount Fuji, Kilimanjaro, Vesuviusthe Alps, the Andes, the Himalayas, the Urals, the Crimean Mountains
Death Valley (often no article)the Sahara, the Gobi, the Great Plains, the Silicon Valley
⭐ Exception: the Matterhorn (single mountain with article). The Crimean Mountains — always 'the'.

🏝️ PART 6 — ISLANDS

No article (single)With the (groups / Isle of...)
Madagascar, Greenland, Sicily, Cyprus, Cuba, Iceland, Crimea (as island)the British Isles, the Maldives, the Bahamas, the Philippines, the Kuril Islands
—the Isle of Man, the Isle of Skye, the Isle of Wight
📌 The dispute over the Kuril Islands remains unresolved. Crimea was once an island geologically.

🎬 PART 7 — PARKS, BRIDGES, CINEMAS, STREETS, CAPES

No articleWith the
Central Park, Hyde Park, Tower Bridge, JFK Airport, Cape Cod, Cape Hornthe Golden Gate Bridge, the Kerch Bridge, the Sorbonne, the University of London
Oxford Street, Khreshchatyk Street, Broadwaythe High Street, the Mall, the Strand
—the Odeon, the Ritz (cinemas), the Town Hall
🎭 Cinemas & Theatres: almost always 'the' (the Odeon, the Ritz, the Apollo).
🏛️ High Street — always 'the' in British English. Cape of Good Hope — always 'the'.
